How to Use Noltrax Track

Build structured football data from raw events to spatial intelligence

01

Event Logger

Record raw match events in sequence. This tool focuses on what happened, not interpretation.

  • Pass
  • Shot
  • Duel
  • Clearance
  • Interception
02

Event Context

Add meaning to raw events by defining outcomes and results.

  • Shot: On / Off Target
  • Duel: Won / Lost
  • Clearance: Success / Failed
  • Pass: Progressive / Safe
03

Spatial Mapping

Assign events into pitch zones to understand where football happens.

  • Half-space
  • Central zone
  • Wide channels
  • Final third
