How It Works

We’ve simplified the analysis workflow into three powerful modules.

MODULE 01

Noltrax Match

Interactive Video Tagging

Analyze matches directly using YouTube integration. Use intuitive event log buttons to tag key moments such as Build-up phases or Counter-attacks in real-time.

Every interaction is automatically timestamped, allowing you to organize observations and plan matchday strategies using the built-in Planner.

MODULE 02

Noltrax Track

Custom Statistical Collection

Take control of the numbers. Record every action—from passes and interceptions to fouls—using customizable keyboard shortcuts.

  • Tactical Shortcuts: Press 1 or 2 to log home team actions, or use the opposite keys for the away side.
  • Live Possession: Toggle the Q key to track real-time possession percentages based on your own live input.
MODULE 03

Noltrax Data

Process & Visualize

Turn raw observations into actionable insights. Every event logged in Match and every statistic recorded in Track is automatically synced here.

Noltrax Data processes your saved files into a clean, structured overview—helping you understand performance patterns beyond the scoreboard.
