NOLTRAX TRACK

Precision Live Statistics

Take full control of the data stream. With customizable tracking cards and intuitive keyboard shortcuts, you can record every pass, interception, and foul in real-time. Our live possession engine ensures your data matches the tempo of the match.

Raw data is only as good as the insights you draw from it. Noltrax Data automatically aggregates logs from Match and Track, cleans them, and turns chaotic spreadsheets into structured datasets ready for reports and presentations.
